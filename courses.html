Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose the right program?

It depends on your schedule, learning style, and test date. The Weekend Program is great for consistent long-term prep, the Intensive Program suits focused learners with more time available, and Self-Study works for self-motivated students. Book a consultation and we'll help you decide!

What is the Score Guarantee Program?

Our Score Guarantee Program ensures eligible students will see score improvement. Specific terms and eligibility criteria apply. Contact us to learn more about qualification requirements.

Can I switch between in-person and online?

Yes! We offer hybrid learning options. You can attend classes in person at our Parsippany location or join online. Discuss your needs during enrollment.

What materials are provided?

All courses include comprehensive study materials, access to the Y2 Online Integrated Study System, practice tests, and additional resources. Everything you need for success is included.

How many students are in each class?

We maintain small class sizes to ensure personalized attention. This allows our instructors to address individual student needs and provide targeted guidance.

When should I start preparing?

Ideally, start 3-6 months before your target test date. However, our Intensive Program can help students who need faster preparation. The earlier you start, the more time you have to improve.
