Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best way to contact you?

For immediate questions, call or WhatsApp us. For detailed inquiries, email works best. To schedule a meeting, use our Calendly booking system.

How quickly will I get a response?

Phone and WhatsApp: Usually within a few hours during business hours. Email: Within 24 hours. Calendly bookings are instant.

Can I tour your facility?

Absolutely! We welcome visitors. Please call ahead or book a consultation to ensure someone is available to show you around.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes! We offer complimentary 30-minute consultations to discuss your SAT preparation needs and recommend the best program for you.

What if I'm outside your service area?

We offer online courses that students from anywhere can attend. Contact us to discuss online options.

How do I enroll in a course?

After your consultation, we'll guide you through the enrollment process. You can also contact us directly if you're ready to enroll.
